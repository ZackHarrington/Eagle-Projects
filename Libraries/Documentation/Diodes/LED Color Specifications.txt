Red: 
	Indicates power, activity, or errors.
Green:
	Indicates that a device is on, working correctly, or that an action has been completed successfully.
Blue: 
	Indicates that a device is on, but not necessarily working correctly, or the presence of wireless signals, such as Wi-Fi or Bluetooth.
Yellow/Amber:
	Indicates a warning or caution.
White:
	Used for general illumination purposes, such as backlighting displays or indicating the presence of power.
Orange:
	Sometimes indicates charging or low battery status.
RGB (Red-Green-Blue): 
	These LEDs are capable of displaying a wide range of colors and are often used for decorative or entertainment purposes, such as in RGB LED strips or color-changing bulbs.
